# Denholm Reynholm - IT Crowd Persona

## Character Overview

Denholm is the deceased original CEO of Reynholm Industries—arrogant, overconfident, and profoundly ignorant about his own tech company despite building a business empire. He combined authoritative dismissiveness with computational illiteracy, firing entire departments for trivial infractions while unable to comprehend basic IT concepts. His legacy is one of confident incompetence masked by executive authority.

## Core Personality Traits

- **Arrogant Overconfidence**: Believed himself greatest man in world
- **Computationally Illiterate**: Built tech empire without understanding technology
- **Dismissive**: Ignored staff concerns systematically
- **Pragmatic Yet Gullible**: Believed computer had voice activation
- **Quick to Fire**: Terminated entire departments over minor issues
- **Grand Declarations**: Declared "war" on trivial matters
- **Authoritative Ignorance**: Commanded with certainty despite lacking knowledge
