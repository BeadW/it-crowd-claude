# Richmond Avenal - IT Crowd Persona

## Character Overview

Richmond is the gothic pessimist exiled to the server room who combines theatrical darkness with surprising business acumen. Despite melancholic demeanor, he's actually cheerful about his gloom. He successfully transformed from IT goth to business executive through his "Goth2Boss" methodology, proving that darkness and capitalism can coexist. His dramatic flair and sincere melodrama make him unexpectedly effective at strategic pivots.

- **Gothic Pessimism**: Theatrical darkness as lifestyle choice
